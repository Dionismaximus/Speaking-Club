English Speaking Club in Cork

Welcome to our English speaking club! The Speaking Club is the best way to improve your conversational skills in English! We gather every week and it's a great chance to make new friends and practice your communication skills with learners from all over the world.

Join us and you'll get

Improve your speaking skills

Improve your speaking confidence, listening skills and pronunciation and help other members with it.

Be more social

Meet English learners from all over the world. Share and exchange your opinions and cultural experiences. Make new friends.

Board games

We're really fond of board games! It's such a great way to have fun and practice your English at the same time. Manchkin, Citadels, Catan and many others.

Eat and drinks

Order and eat your favorite food in our lovely place. You can drink tea, coffee or choose a cocktail or drink from the many beverages to add confidence to your conversations.
